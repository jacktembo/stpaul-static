List of Successful Candidates admitted into Post Basic Nursing Programme – 2021 Intake

By Ibrahim Zakariyau / In News / March 5, 2021

 

This is to inform all successful candidates admitted into POST BASIC NURSING programme at Kasimu Kofar Bai School of Nursing, st. Paul that ONLINE Registration will commence on Monday, 8th March, 2021 and ends on Friday 19th March, 2021.

  • Method of Online Registration
    • Visit www.st. Paul.edu.ng
    • Click on STUDENTS REGISTRATION PORTAL;
    • Click on REGISTRATION GUIDELINES to read and follow the guideline for the online registration.
  • All successful candidates MUST present to Kasimu Kofar Bai School of Nursing st. Paul, the ORIGINAL and 3 copies of the under-listed:
  1. Admission Letter (To be printed online, COLOURED and on a cream CONQUEROR Paper)
    1. Course Registration Form
    2. Payment Slip
    3. Completed Acceptance Form
    4. Completed ID Card Form
    5. Completed Undertaking Letter
    6. Completed Guarantorâ€™s Form
    7. Completed Library Form
    8. Six (6) Passport Photographs
    9. Acknowledgement Card
    10. NMCN Licence
    11. Notification of Registration
    12. Certificate of Registration
    13. SSCE Certificate or Statement of Result
    14. Secondary School Testimonial
    15. Primary Certificate
    16. Primary School Testimonial
    17. Indigene Letter
    18. Result Scratch Card(s)

NOTE: Item 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7 can be printed on the Registration Portal

  • Failure to register by Friday 19th March, 2021 means forfeiture of admission.
  • Matriculation Ceremony: Monday, 22nd March, 2021.
  • Commencement of Lecture: Tuesday, 23rd March, 2021
